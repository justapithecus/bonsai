# CONTRACT_OBSERVATIONAL.md

## Purpose
Constrain Grove to factual observation without interpretation.

## Scope
All data collection, summaries, and presentation layers.

## Invariants
- Observations are descriptive and time-bound.
- Inputs and methods are traceable to observed sources.
- Observations do not imply intent, cause, or value.

## Prohibitions
- No inference of motives, priorities, or goals.
- No attribution of meaning beyond what is directly observed.
- No synthesis that converts observations into judgments.

## Allowed Outputs
- Counts, diffs, timelines, and structural inventories.
- Contextual notes that only restate what is observed.
